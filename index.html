BEVSTUBBAL.

11.06.2022

One year on; love still strong

the countdown

In 2021, we finally tied the knot but due to the infamous coronavirus, we couldn't celebrate with all our lovely family and friends. Lets right that wrong shall we?

Join us on the 11th June 2022 to celebrate our one year anniversary with a Beverton-Tubbs festival at Orchard House (the same venue we got married). Here's all the key info you need to know but if you have any other questions, give us a shout.

Key information

  • Dresscode

    Think smart summer BBQ.

  • Camping

    There will be a camping area to pitch a tent for those who want to spend the night. We'll be spending a night under the stars, let us know if you're joining us.

  • Parking

    It's a private property with plenty of space. Overnight is no problem.

  • Taxis

    Must be booked in advance. Local firms: Andrew’s 01702 200200, Alpine 01702 531531, Village 01702 207222 or AC 01702 334455

  • RSVP

    Just let us know via text please (no fancy forms this time).

  • Gifts

    Not necessary. This is just a big party to see you all and celebrate so your presence is all we desire.

  • Food

    Street food. All dietary requirements catered for but just ask if you're worried about it.

  • Drinks

    It's an open bar so no need to bring anything.

  • Kids

    This will be a child-friendly event so feel free to bring your kids if you wish or get childcare and go nuts; your call :)

Line-up

  • 15:00Arrival, drinks & garden games

    Head on down, pitch a tent (if you're camping), grab a drink and enjoy some garden games in the sun*.
    *Weather not guaranteed.

  • 15:30Garden stage: Hummingbird

    Hummingbird kick things off on the garden stage with some acoustic renditions of some massive hits.

  • 18:00Street food

    We'll be having an eclectic mix of exciting street food to cater for all dietary needs.

  • 19:00Welcome toast

    Don't worry, we're not doing 30 mins of wedding speeches but we would like to say a few words to welcome everyone and mark the occasion.

  • 19:30Main stage: Cèilidh

    Time to enjoy some organised dancing with Stumpy Oak so no excuses; we want to see everyone up there spinning their partners and getting their cardio in.

  • 22:00Silent disco

    Grab a pair of headphones and throw away what's left of your inhibitions because it's time to dance the remainder of the night away to 3 channels of carefully curated music from the Beverton-Tubbses.

  • 00:00Last orders

    All good things must come to an end. Midnight seems like the logical time. Hopefully for those camping, they remembered to pitch their tent before they drunk too much and the sun set.
