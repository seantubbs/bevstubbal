One year on; love still strong

the countdown

In 2021, we finally tied the knot but due to the infamous coronavirus, we couldn't celebrate with all our lovely family and friends. Lets right that wrong shall we?

Join us on the 11th June 2022 to celebrate our one year anniversary with a Beverton-Tubbs festival at Orchard House (the same venue we got married). Here's all the key info you need to know but if you have any other questions, give us a shout.

Key information

Dresscode: It's a festival so just some comfortable and dressed to party
Camping: There will be a camping area to pitch a tent for those who want to spend the night
RSVP: Just let us know via text please (no fancy forms this time)
Gifts: Not necessary. This is just a big party to see you all and celebrate so your presence is all we need
Drinks: It's an open bar so no need to bring anything
Kids: This will be a child-friendly event so feel free to bring your kids if you wish or get childcare and go nuts; your call :)
